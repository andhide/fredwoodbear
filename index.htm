Sleepy Ed Hicks was born of mistake and misunderstanding. Through his playful banjo antics he brings together Old Time American Songs, hard and fast Appalachian Dance Music and Witty Novelty numbers into a tight set that never fails to please.

Touring solo round the country he uses his Banjo virtuosity, warm friendly baritone voice and good humour to take the music of the banjo to audiences of all kinds.

On this site you can delve into the mysterious origins of Sleepy Ed Hicks in the BanjoMan comic, check for forth-coming performances in the live section and view photos of Ed and various other old time players.

Sleepy Ed Hicks is available for performances in venues through out the country and for Banjo Tuition and Session work in the areas of London and Bristol. To contact Ed please click here.
